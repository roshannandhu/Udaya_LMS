Privacy Policy

Udaya LMS · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Udaya LMS application and website (“Udaya”, “we”, “us”) collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected. By using Udaya you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Udaya is a learning-management platform operated by Udaya Centre for its enrolled students, their parents/guardians, and teaching staff. For any privacy question or request, contact us at udayatuitionhome@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Account & profile: name, phone number, login identifier, profile photo (optional).
  • Academic data: class/standard, subjects, test scores and marks, attendance records, assignment submissions.
  • Learning activity: videos watched, broadcasts read, app usage needed to run the service.
  • Files you upload: e.g. assignment files, photos.
  • Technical data: basic device/connection information used for security and to operate the app.

Student accounts are created by the teacher/institution; students do not self-register.

3. How we use it

  • To provide the LMS: classes, videos, tests, attendance, reports, and announcements.
  • To let teachers manage students and let parents/students view progress.
  • To secure accounts (e.g. single-device login, verification) and prevent misuse.
  • To send service communications (e.g. credentials, notifications) where applicable.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Where your data is stored

Data is processed and stored using reputable cloud providers: application database and authentication on Supabase, backend compute on Amazon Web Services (AWS), and files/backups on Cloudflare R2. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

5. Children’s data & parental consent

Udaya is used by school students, who may be minors. Accounts for minors are created and managed by the institution with the consent of a parent/guardian, who may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child’s data at any time using the contact below.

6. Data retention

We retain personal and academic data for as long as the student is enrolled and as needed for legitimate educational and record-keeping purposes. On a verified deletion request, we remove the associated personal data except where retention is required by law.

7. Your rights

You (or a parent/guardian for a minor) may request to access, correct, or delete personal data, or withdraw consent, by emailing udayatuitionhome@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable period.

8. Security

We apply technical and organisational measures including encrypted transport, access controls, and regular backups. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

10. Contact

Udaya Centre · udayatuitionhome@gmail.com
