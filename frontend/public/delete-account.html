Delete your account and data

Udaya Learn · Last updated: July 2026

Udaya Learn is a private tuition learning app operated by Udaya Centre. Student accounts are created and managed by the teacher/institution. This page explains how a student, or their parent/guardian, can request deletion of the account and its associated data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to the address below from any email address, asking us to delete the account. So we can find and verify the correct account, please include:

  • The Student ID (for example 25UDAYA100001), or the student’s full name and class/standard.
  • A short note confirming you are the student or their parent/guardian.

Email us to delete your account

Or email udayatuitionhome@gmail.com directly.

What gets deleted

On a verified request we permanently delete the account and the personal data associated with it, including:

  • Account & profile: name, phone number, login identifier and profile photo.
  • Academic data: test scores and marks, attendance records and assignment submissions.
  • Learning activity and files you uploaded (for example assignment files and photos).
  • The device identifier used for single-device login and the push-notification token.

What may be retained

We may retain a limited amount of information where retention is required by law, or to resolve disputes and prevent misuse. Any retained data is kept only for as long as necessary and is then deleted. We do not sell your personal data or use it for third-party advertising.

Timeframe

We action verified deletion requests within 30 days. If we need more information to verify the request, we will contact you at the email address you wrote from.
